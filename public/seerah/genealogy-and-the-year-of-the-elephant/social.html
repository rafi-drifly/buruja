Day 5 of 104.

The seerah proper begins: twenty grandfathers, one protected House, and an army of elephants.
01 / 08
His lineage ﷺ back to Adnan: twenty names, agreed upon, memorized for fourteen centuries.

Beyond Adnan? Our scholars had the honesty to say: Allah knows best.
02 / 08
The Prophet ﷺ said: Allah chose Kinanah from the children of Ismail, Quraysh from Kinanah, Banu Hashim from Quraysh, and chose me from Banu Hashim.

Choice within choice within choice.
03 / 08
Qusayy took the city back and fed its pilgrims.
Hashim plugged Makkah into the Silk Road and never ate alone.
Abd al-Muttalib found Zamzam with a dream, a shovel, and one son.

Every grandfather built something. None of it was random.
04 / 08
570 CE. An army marches on the Ka'bah behind war elephants.

The chief of Makkah asks only for his camels back, then says: I am the lord of the camels. The House has its own Lord, and He will protect it.
05 / 08
The lead elephant knelt at the edge of Makkah.

They whipped him, beat him, bled him. He would not take one step toward the House.

Then the sky filled with birds: "Striking them with stones of hard clay, And He made them like eaten straw." (Surah al-Fil 105:4-5)
06 / 08
And in the city below, a young wife named Aminah was expecting.

The Prophet ﷺ was in her womb the day Allah defended His House.

He was not only guarding a building.
07 / 08
Born a few months later, in that same Year of the Elephant.

Day 6: the birth of the Prophet ﷺ. The chain was complete; now the story begins.
