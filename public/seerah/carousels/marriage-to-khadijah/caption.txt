Day 9 of the seerah: the last two stories before the revelation. A wealthy, much-courted woman chose a poor shepherd for one reason: he was honest when no one important was watching. Khadijah radiyallahu anha believed in him first, gave to him first, comforted him first, and bore him all but one of his children. He never stopped loving her. Then, at thirty-five, when his whole city nearly drew swords over who would set the Black Stone back in the Ka'bah, he settled it with a cloak and four pairs of hands, and every tribe rejoiced that it was him. Before a single ayah was revealed, everyone already trusted him completely.
