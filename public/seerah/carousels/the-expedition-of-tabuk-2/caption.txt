Day 88 of 104: the second part of Tabuk, and the story of Kab ibn Malik, the man who would not lie his way out. He had every reason to march and stayed home anyway. When the moment came, he told the Prophet ﷺ the truth and accepted fifty nights as a stranger in his own city, even an enemy king's escape route burned in his own oven. A story about procrastination, honesty, and the mercy waiting past the punishment. Retold faithfully from Dr. Yasir Qadhi's seerah series (episode embedded on the site), Qur'an verified via quran.ai.
