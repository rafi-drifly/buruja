Day 87 of the seerah: the army of hardship. The Prophet's ﷺ final campaign, and there was no battle in it at all, only the march. A thousand miles in the July heat, the harvest abandoned, men sharing a single date and nearly dying of thirst until the sky opened over Abu Bakr's du'a. Uthman emptied a caravan for it; Abu Bakr left his family Allah and His Messenger. They moved on Allah's word with no enemy promised at the end, and Allah named them in the Qur'an as those who followed him in the hour of difficulty.
