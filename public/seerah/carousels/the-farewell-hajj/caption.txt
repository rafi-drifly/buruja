Day 100 of 104: the Farewell Hajj. He ﷺ made the pilgrimage exactly once, stood at Arafah with his hands raised until sunset, and there the verse came down that the religion was perfected (al-Ma'idah 5:3). In his sermon he abolished the feuds of the old days starting with his own family, defended the rights of women, declared all people equal but for piety, and left the ummah the Book of Allah. Then he asked them to bear witness that he had delivered it. A goodbye dressed as a triumph.
