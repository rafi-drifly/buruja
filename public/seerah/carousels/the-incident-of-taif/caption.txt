Day 20 of 104: the incident of Ta'if. Weeks after the Year of Sorrow, the Prophet ﷺ walks into the mountains, is rejected and stoned, and makes the du'a that has carried the brokenhearted ever since. Offered the destruction of the city that bloodied him, he refuses, and prays instead for their children. He named it the hardest day of his life, and it is one of the greatest displays of his mercy.
