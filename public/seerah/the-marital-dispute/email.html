The one month he ﷺ withdrew

Preview: Day 98: the single marital dispute of his entire life, and the verse that asked his wives to choose.

Assalamu alaykum,

In the whole of his life ﷺ, the perfect husband, there was exactly one season when the strain at home grew serious enough that he withdrew. Once. And Allah sent down Qur'an because of it.

Dr. Yasir Qadhi approaches it gently, and so will we. As wealth began to flow into the ummah, the wives of the Prophet ﷺ, the mothers of the believers, asked for a better standard of living. Hold this clearly: what they asked for was halal, the most natural wish in the world, and it took nothing away from their noble rank. But he ﷺ had chosen, for our sake, to live with almost nothing.

So he withdrew for one month to a small room above the masjid. When Umar finally entered it, he found a handful of barley, a skin of water, and a palm-fiber mat that had pressed its marks into the Prophet's ﷺ own side. The most honored man on earth, living like this. Umar wept and said: make du'a for more, the Persians and Romans live in luxury. And the Prophet ﷺ answered: are you in doubt, O Umar? Are you not pleased that they have this world, and we have the next?

Then came the verse of choice. If you want this world, he was told to tell his wives, take it, with a gracious release. But if you want Allah and His Messenger ﷺ and the Hereafter, a great reward awaits. Aisha did not even pause: what is there to consult about? Every one of them chose Allah and His Messenger ﷺ, though a fortune was within reach.

The best marriage in history was not the one without storms. It was the one whose single storm was weathered so beautifully that we are still learning from it.
