Juz Amma · Surah 114
An-Nas
The last surah of the Qur'an, where the Book that named every enemy turns at last to the one inside you, and hands you the only door out
buruja.comcover
This is the last page of the Qur'an.

The Book that named every great enemy ends on the smallest one: a voice whispering inside your own chest.
buruja.com01 / 08
The surah before it sought refuge from outside evils: the dark, the magicians, the envier.

But those can only harm this life.

So the Qur'an saves the worst for last.
buruja.com02 / 08
Al-Falaq asks for refuge through ONE name of Allah from many evils.

An-Nas asks through THREE (Rabb, Malik, Ilah) from a single one.

That is how grave this one is.
buruja.com03 / 08
He is called al-waswas, the whisperer.

But he is also al-khannas, the one who RETREATS.

Hidden in his name is his weakness.
buruja.com04 / 08
When does a star come out? At night.

The shaytan shows up when the light of remembrance has gone out, and slips back the moment it returns.

The cure is in the word: dhikr.
buruja.com05 / 08
Notice the mercy: the whisper lands in the CHEST, not yet the heart.

The heart is a castle. The chest is the field around it.

While you keep watch, he only circles the wall.
buruja.com06 / 08
Watch what you love.

A believer finds beauty in faith and disgust at sin.

When sin starts to look sweet and good feels heavy, he is closer than you think.
buruja.com07 / 08
The cure for the whisper is remembrance.

You will not be rid of him. You keep him stepping back, every day.

Surat An-Nas, retold from Sheikh Abu Bakr Zoud.
