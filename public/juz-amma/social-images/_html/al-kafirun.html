The surah of clean break, where a man with nothing left to fear and nothing left to gain says no to a deal, six times over

Quraysh had tried everything to make the Prophet ﷺ stop preaching.

Money. Marriage. Leadership. Even doctors.

None of it worked. So they offered a deal.

'Worship our gods for one year,' they said, 'and we will worship your Lord the next.'

Everyone gives a little. Everyone goes home happy.
buruja.com02 / 08
Sheikh Abu Bakr Zoud names the trap.

It was never about a year of worship. It was about getting him to give, just a little, so the whole thing could be loosened.
buruja.com03 / 08
Picture a full water tank.

You don't need to smash it to empty it. One small hole, and it drains until it's dry.

Let one piece of your faith be poked, and the rest begins to leak.

The answer came down in six ayat: Al-Kafirun.

'I do not worship what you worship. Nor will I ever. Nor will you worship what I worship.'

The present and the future. Not today, not tomorrow.

Notice he's told to say it OUT LOUD, to their faces.

When YOU are insulted: be patient, let Allah defend you.

When ALLAH is dishonored: you stand, and you speak.

The believer carries his through the hardship and won't trade away a single piece.
