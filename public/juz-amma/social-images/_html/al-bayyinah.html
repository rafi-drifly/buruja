Juz Amma · Surah 98
Al-Bayyinah
The surah that names the one thing that should have settled everything, and then shows you why people split apart anyway
buruja.comcover
Surat Al-Bayyinah opens on people who were completely stuck.

Allah says nothing would pry them loose, not argument, not time, until ONE thing arrived.

A clear proof.
buruja.com01 / 08
What is a 'clear proof'?

Sheikh Abu Bakr explains it with a courtroom. The judge tells the accuser one thing: bring your proof.

The moment real proof is on the table, the case is over.
buruja.com02 / 08
So what was the proof?

Not a sign in the sky. A person.

The final Messenger ﷺ, reciting purified pages. The case should have closed right there.
buruja.com03 / 08
Then the line that stops you.

The People of the Book divided only AFTER the proof reached them.

Not from confusion. From jealousy. He came from outside their circle.
buruja.com04 / 08
Two men. Same proof.

One, the most learned of them, bowed, and was instantly called 'the worst of them.'

The other, about to be crowned king, never forgave losing his crown.
buruja.com05 / 08
The whole command was simple.

Worship Allah sincerely, lean toward the truth, pray, give.

The cure for their envy was the first word: SINCERE. The sincere have nothing to be jealous over.
buruja.com06 / 08
Be a slave, not just a worshipper.

A worshipper clocks in for one set hour, then off duty.

A slave of Allah belongs to Him around the clock. No corner of the day fenced off.
buruja.com07 / 08
Seeing the truth is not the same as bowing to it.

The same man the deniers called the worst, Allah named the best of creation.

Surat Al-Bayyinah, retold from Sheikh Abu Bakr Zoud.
