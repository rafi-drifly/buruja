Juz Amma · Surah 97
Al-Qadr
The surah that names the one night Allah hid from you on purpose, and tells you it is worth more than a lifetime
buruja.comcover
Surat Al-Qadr is five short ayat about a single night.

The strange thing about it is what it refuses to tell you.
buruja.com01 / 08
It tells you the whole Qur'an came down in this night.

Not piece by piece. The entire Book, all at once, from the highest heaven to the lowest.
buruja.com02 / 08
It tells you one night of worship is 'better than a thousand months.'

That is more than 83 years. Longer than you will live.

Allah is offering you, in one night, a bonus larger than your whole life.
buruja.com03 / 08
It tells you the angels pour down from the sky.

And they seek their Lord's permission just to come and greet YOU by name.

In that night, you are the one worth visiting.
buruja.com04 / 08
It tells you the whole night is nothing but peace.

Every other night can carry some harm. This one cannot.

Peace, start to finish, until the break of dawn.
buruja.com05 / 08
And then it hides the one thing you want to know: which night it is.

Why? So laziness can't let you sleep through a known date. And so no one carries the guilt of wasting a night he was told about.
buruja.com06 / 08
Even the secret is a mercy.

Allah told us only this: it falls on one of the odd nights of the last ten.

So take all of them seriously.
buruja.com07 / 08
The surah is not about a calendar.

It is about whether you will be awake and standing when the night passes over you.

Surat Al-Qadr, retold from Sheikh Abu Bakr Zoud.
