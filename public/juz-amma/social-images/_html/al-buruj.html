Buruja
Juz Amma · Surah 85
Al-Buruj
The surah that answers a tortured believer with the stars over his head, a buried story of fire, and the love of the One on the Throne
buruja.comcover
Surat Al-Buruj was sent to people being tortured for their faith.

It does not open by promising them rescue. It opens with the night sky over their heads.
buruja.com01 / 08
Allah calls the stars buruj: fortresses.

And a fortress holds soldiers. The soldiers of the sky are the angels, armed and ready above you.

You are not alone.
buruja.com02 / 08
Then a story: the people of the ditch.

A tyrant dug trenches, filled them with fire, and burned alive everyone who would not abandon their faith.
buruja.com03 / 08
The boy in the story could have run and lived a dozen times.

He kept walking back to the king.

He was not chasing his own survival. He was chasing a whole town for Allah.
buruja.com04 / 08
They were hated for one thing only: that they believed in Allah.

The word for what was done to them, fitna, means putting gold in the fire until only the pure is left.
buruja.com05 / 08
Watch the missing word.

The tormentors get their fire AS A RESULT. The believers get gardens with no 'because' at all.

The Fire is earned. The Garden never is.
buruja.com06 / 08
Then the turn. Allah's grip on the tyrant is severe, escape-proof.

And in the same breath He is al-Wadud: the One who loves you INTENSELY, from the glorious Throne.
buruja.com07 / 08
Your strength is in this Qur'an.

Allah promised protection not to the believers, but to the Book. A mission guarded forever cannot fail.

Surat Al-Buruj, retold from Sheikh Abu Bakr Zoud.
