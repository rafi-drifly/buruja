Juz Amma

The thirtieth juz, surah by surah

A daily series

All surahs

37 of 37 surahs of Juz Amma, each with a read, an email, and a social carousel. Retold faithfully from Sheikh Abu Bakr Zoud's tafsir of Juz Amma. Qur'an verified.

78. An-Naba
The surah that opens on a question the deniers were whispering, and an answer that will not let you sit still
79. An-Nazi'at
The surah that opens on angels ripping souls loose, and closes on a Day so near it shrinks your whole life to an afternoon
80. Abasa
The surah where Allah corrects His own beloved Messenger over a single frown, and teaches you who is really worth your time
81. At-Takwir
The surah that switches off the sun, drops the stars, and undoes the world, then turns and asks you what could possibly be worth chasing instead of the truth in your hands
82. Al-Infitar
The surah that splits the sky open above your head, then turns and asks you, to your face, what could possibly have fooled you about your generous Lord
83. Al-Mutaffifin
The surah that begins with woe over a few stolen grams, and ends with two records, one sunk in the lowest pit and one raised to the highest heaven
84. Al-Inshiqaq
The surah where the sky tears open and obeys, the earth flattens and empties, and you are told that all your toil has only ever been a walk toward your Lord
85. Al-Buruj
The surah that answers a tortured believer with the stars over his head, a buried story of fire, and the love of the One on the Throne
86. At-Tariq
The surah that swears by a star knocking through the dark, and turns it into proof that the One who made you can bring you back
87. Al-A'la
The surah the Prophet ﷺ loved, that opens by lifting the name of your Lord above every defect and closes by asking which life you actually prefer
88. Al-Ghashiyah
The surah that names the Day after the way it will cover you, and then asks you to look at a camel
89. Al-Fajr
The surah that swears by the first light, walks you past the ruins of the mightiest men who ever lived, and ends with a soul called home
90. Al-Balad
The surah that swears by Makkah, tells you that you were made for struggle, and then points to one steep road worth climbing
91. Ash-Shams
The surah that swears by the sun seven times over to teach you the one thing you can ruin or rescue: your own soul
92. Al-Layl
The surah that swears by the night and the day, then tells you that everything you are running toward splits into just two roads
93. Ad-Duha
The surah Allah sent down when the revelation had gone quiet and His Prophet ﷺ was grieving, to tell him he was never once abandoned
94. Ash-Sharh
The surah where Allah, fresh off comforting His Messenger ﷺ, keeps counting the gifts, and lands on a promise you can lean your whole weight against
95. At-Tin
The surah that swears by a fruit, an oil, and two holy mountains, and lands on what you were really built to be
96. Al-Alaq
The surah of the very first revelation, where the first word God ever sent down was an order to read, given to a man who could not
97. Al-Qadr
The surah that names the one night Allah hid from you on purpose, and tells you it is worth more than a lifetime
98. Al-Bayyinah
The surah that names the one thing that should have settled everything, and then shows you why people split apart anyway
99. Az-Zalzalah
The surah where the ground you trusted turns witness, and an atom's weight of anything is finally seen
100. Al-Adiyat
The surah that paints a war horse so loyal it will die for you, then asks why you will not even live for your Lord
101. Al-Qari'ah
The surah that names the Day after a sound, a single word that strikes you, then refuses to tell you what it is until you have read to the end
102. At-Takathur
The surah that catches you mid-count, busy piling up a world you will leave, and asks what all that counting was really for
103. Al-Asr
The three short ayat that, ash-Shafi'i said, would have been enough on their own to guide mankind
104. Al-Humazah
The surah that curses the man who crushed people with his tongue and hoarded his wealth, and shows you the Fire built to crush him back
105. Al-Fil
The surah where an army big enough to flatten a city is undone by birds, and the proudest plan in Arabia is turned into chewed straw
106. Quraysh
The only surah named after a tribe, naming the four favors that should have bought their worship, and asking for it back
107. Al-Ma'un
The surah that measures your faith not by what you say, but by how you treat the orphan, the poor, and the small thing your neighbor asks to borrow
108. Al-Kawthar
The shortest surah in the Qur'an, handed to the Prophet ﷺ on the night they called him cut off, a river of abundance against a single insult
109. Al-Kafirun
The surah of clean break, where a man with nothing left to fear and nothing left to gain says no to a deal, six times over
110. An-Nasr
The surah that arrived as a triumph and carried, folded inside the congratulation, the gentlest notice that a life's work was done
111. Al-Masad
The surah where Allah Himself answers an uncle who raised his hands against His Messenger, and names him, and his wife, into the Fire
112. Al-Ikhlas
The surah that answers one question, who is your Lord, and the Prophet ﷺ said its four lines weigh a third of the whole Qur'an
113. Al-Falaq
The first of the two surahs of refuge, where you cling to the Lord who splits the dawn and ask Him to tear through every darkness in your life
114. An-Nas
The last surah of the Qur'an, where the Book that named every enemy turns at last to the one inside you, and hands you the only door out
