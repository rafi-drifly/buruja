Day 23 of 29: Musa and the Children of Israel, part 2. Three scenes the Qur'an froze in place, the cow they argued into a mountain (al-Baqarah 2:67-73), Qarun's wealth swallowed by the earth (al-Qasas 28:76-82), and the day Allah humbled Musa by sending him to learn from al-Khidr (al-Kahf 18:60-82). One lesson underneath all three: when Allah commands, obey, instead of arguing it into something heavy. A nation was handed more proof than any people in history, and still the hardest thing was simply to obey, the same test that meets us in every prayer and every act of giving.
