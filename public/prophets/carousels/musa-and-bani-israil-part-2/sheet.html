A man was murdered. The community was tearing itself apart.

Allah's answer: slaughter a cow. Any cow. The matter ends today.

Instead, they started asking questions.

What kind of cow? What colour? Describe it exactly.

With every question the description tightened, until only one impossible cow on earth would do.

They made it difficult, so Allah made it difficult for them.
