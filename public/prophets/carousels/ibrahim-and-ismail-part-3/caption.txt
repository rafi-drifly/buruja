Day 12 of 29: the strangest test in the whole series. Not a fire, not an idol, not a king, but a command to leave a wife and a newborn in an empty valley and walk away, on nothing but the word of Allah. Hajar ran seven times between Safa and Marwah, and Zamzam burst from the sand, still flowing today. When you walk the sa'i on Hajj, you are retracing her exact steps. The pilgrimage you will one day perform is this family's submission, turned into living ritual, and from this child came the line that led to the Prophet ﷺ.
