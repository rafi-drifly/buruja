Day 27 of 29: Sulayman, peace be upon him, the king Solomon. He inherits his father Dawud, prays for a kingdom no one after him would have, and is given the wind and the jinn. Then watch what the most powerful man who ever lived does with all of it: he stops his army for an ant, refuses a queen's bribe, and when a throne crosses an empire in a blink, he calls it a test of whether he will be grateful. The kingdom was never his; it was lent by al-Malik, the King. Retold faithfully from Mufti Ismail Menk's Stories of the Prophets (the episode is embedded on the site), Qur'an verified via quran.ai.
