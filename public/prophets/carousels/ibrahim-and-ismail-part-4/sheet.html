Twice in one life a father stood over a son with death between them.

The first time, a fire. The second time, a knife in his own hand.

The difference is the whole lesson.

First scene: the father disbelieved, and lost. The believing son was saved.

Second scene: father and son both submitted. So both of them won.

The only one who ever loses is Shaytan.

Then father and son knelt in an empty valley and raised the Ka'bah, stone by stone.

And as they built, Ibrahim asked: show us our rites.

He built the house, and still would not invent a single act of worship. He asked to be taught.
