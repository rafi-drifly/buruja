The most powerful king who ever lived died standing up.

Sulayman, peace be upon him, leaned on his staff. The jinn worked in front of him, terrified.

His soul was taken. His body stayed standing.

They kept labouring for days, for a man who was already gone.

04 / 10
Only a small creature, gnawing the staff until it broke, brought him down.

So the next time someone claims to know your future:

the jinn could not see that the man in front of them was dead. (Saba 34:14)

Then a corridor of prophets.

Ilyas, against an idol named Baal: how can you call on this, and leave the best of creators?

Dhul-Kifl, named among the best in a single line. Sometimes a single line is enough.

06 / 10
An old man. Weak bones. Hair gone white. A wife who could not bear children.

Zakariyya saw out-of-season fruit in Maryam's prayer niche, and something cracked open.

If Allah can do that, he thought, then maybe me too.
buruja.com
07 / 10
He did not announce it. He did not complain to anyone.

He whispered. A private call, just him and his Lord.

'My bones have weakened, my head is aflame with grey, and never once have You left me disappointed.' (Maryam 19:4)

08 / 10
Allah answered, personally.

'O Zakariyya, We give you good news of a boy. His name is Yahya. We have given that name to no one before.'

'How?' he asked. 'It is easy for Me, for I created you before, while you were nothing.'
