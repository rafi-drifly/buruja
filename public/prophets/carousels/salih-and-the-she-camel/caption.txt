Day 9 of 29: Salih and the she-camel. Thamud carved fortresses into the mountains and thought nothing could touch them, so they demanded a sign, and Allah brought a she-camel out of the rock. They hamstrung her over a drink of water, were given three days, and a single blast of sound left them prone in their stone palaces. The whole story is sealed in Surah ash-Shams, the surah whose famous oath you may already know.
