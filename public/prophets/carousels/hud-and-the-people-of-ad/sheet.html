Hud and the people of Ad

The mightiest nation Allah ever made, and the wind that left no trace of them

Day 8 of 29.

The strongest nation Allah ever created.

Giants in the sand dunes, with gardens and springs and palaces carved into the mountaintops.

They had one question they were sure of: who is stronger than us?

This is the people of Ad.

03 / 10
Allah made them successors after the people of Nuh, and increased them in stature beyond anyone before.

They were so tall their homes were built with ceilings a giant would not stoop under.

And they forgot the One who gave it all.
buruja.com
04 / 10
So Allah sent them a brother: Hud, peace be upon him.

One of their own. Known to them his whole life. Truthful, trustworthy.

With one message, the message of every prophet:

O my people, worship Allah. You have no god other than Him.

05 / 10
They answered with insults.

You are a fool. You are possessed. The gods have struck your mind. You are a liar after our money.

Not because they could not understand him.

Because obeying him meant changing their whole way of life.

06 / 10
On every hilltop they built a monument to boast over.

That one is mine. That one is my brother's.

And in the valleys, homes they lived in as if they would never die.

When Allah gives you more, come lower, not higher.

07 / 10
Then came the sentence that named the disease.

"Who is mightier than us in strength?"

Did they not see that the One who created them is mightier than them? (Surah Fussilat 41:15)

The hand that made your muscle is stronger than your muscle.

08 / 10
A great cloud rolled toward their valleys, and the thirsty people cheered: rain, at last.

No. It was a wind carrying a painful punishment.

A screaming, violent wind, seven nights and eight days, until the giants lay like uprooted palm stumps. (Surah al-Haqqah 69:6-8)

09 / 10
Of Pharaoh, a body remains. Of Thamud, ruins remain.

Of Ad, Allah asks: do you see of them any remains?

The proudest nation that ever lived was erased so completely it left less than the weakest.

Iram of the pillars. Strength was no shield.
