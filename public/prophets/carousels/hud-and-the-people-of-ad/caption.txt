Day 8 of 29: Hud and the people of Ad. They were the mightiest nation Allah ever made, giants who carved palaces into the hilltops and asked 'who is stronger than us?' Their brother Hud, peace be upon him, called them to the one God; they mocked him as a fool and a liar; and a screaming wind blew for seven nights and eight days until nothing was left of them to find. They are Iram of the pillars in Surah al-Fajr, the very passage the Prophet ﷺ recited to Quraysh, and a warning that no strength is a shield from the One who made it.
