Day 10 of 29: Ibrahim, the friend of Allah, peace be upon him, part 1. A boy in Babylon reasons his way to the One God while his own father carves idols for a living, smashes the idols and tells the people to ask the big one, and walks out of a fire Allah made cool and safe. The pure tawhid he worked out alone is the very shahada you say today, and the family line that begins with him ends in the Prophet ﷺ.
