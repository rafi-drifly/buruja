Day 7 of 29: Nuh and the flood. He called his people to the one God for 950 years and only a handful believed, then Allah told him to build an ark on a mountain while the chiefs laughed at the 'carpenter.' His patience is the patience the Prophet ﷺ would one day carry in Makkah; his du'a for boarding is one you can say tonight; and after Adam, every human alive descends from him.
