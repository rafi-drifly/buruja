Day 11 of 29: Ibrahim, the friend of Allah, part 2. A sound heart that out-argued a self-proclaimed god, reasoned its way past the star, the moon and the sun, and feared no one but its Maker, even when a tyrant tried to take Sarah and Allah Himself stepped in to protect her. The word he left behind, la ilaha illa Allah, ran straight down his line to the Prophet ﷺ, who is also called Allah's khalil, his intimate friend.
