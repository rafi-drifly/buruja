Day 26 of 29: Dawud, peace be upon him, given a kingdom and kept grateful. He was a king, a prophet, a judge, and a warrior, with a scripture of his own, mountains and birds that glorified Allah along with him, and iron soft in his hands. And still he fasted every other day and prayed a third of every night, the worship the Prophet ﷺ named the most beloved to Allah. We strip away the foreign stories that slander a prophet and keep only what the Qur'an says (Surah Sad 38, Saba 34, al-Anbiya 21), verified via quran.ai. Retold faithfully from Mufti Ismail Menk's beloved series (the episode is embedded on the site). The honest measure of gratitude is not what we say after His gifts, but what we do with the night.
