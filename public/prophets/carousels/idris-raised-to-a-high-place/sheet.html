Where does he fit?

The seventh generation from Adam, the majority say. A prophet between Adam and Nuh.

But almost everything else about him lives outside the Qur'an.

So Mufti Menk hands you a tool.

The Israiliyyat, the stories of the People of the Book, come in three grades:

rejected by revelation, confirmed by revelation, or neither.
buruja.com
05 / 10
The great bulk is 'neither.'

We do not believe it. We do not call it a lie.

We listen, we take a lesson, and we say: Allahu a'lam. Allah knows best.

What IS certain? Two passages.

'Indeed, he was a man of truth and a prophet. And We raised him to a high station.' (Surah Maryam 19:56-57)

Truthful. A prophet. Raised high.

07 / 10
And in Surah al-Anbiya:

Idris, with Ismail and Dhul-Kifl, 'all were of the patient... We admitted them into Our mercy.'

Add patient. Add righteous. The portrait is complete.

Then the moment it all climbs toward.

On the Mi'raj, the Night Journey, the Prophet ﷺ was taken up through the heavens.

And in the fourth heaven, he met Idris. (Sahih al-Bukhari)
