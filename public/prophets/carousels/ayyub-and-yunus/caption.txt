Day 18 of 29: Ayyub and Yunus, peace be upon them. One loses everything piece by piece and never stops thanking Allah. The other is swallowed by the whale in three layers of darkness and calls out the one du'a Allah promised to answer for anyone who says it. Whether your trial is long and grinding or sharp and sudden, the way out is the same: name your Lord, name your own state, and trust His mercy. Say it tonight: la ilaha illa anta, subhanaka, inni kuntu mina az-zalimin.
