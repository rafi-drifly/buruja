Day 14 of 29: Lut, peace be upon him, Ibrahim's nephew, sent to a people who invented a sin no nation had committed and made it the law of the land. He never renamed it to make it easier to live beside. He lost his wife to it, nearly lost his angel-guests to it, and in the end it was the towns that were overturned and his name that Allah kept. The lesson at the centre: halal and haram are Allah's to define, never the crowd's, and a whole world calling a sin normal does not change it. One more rehearsal of the patience the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ would show in Makkah.
