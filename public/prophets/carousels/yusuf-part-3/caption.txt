Day 17 of 29: Yusuf, part 3, the climax and the reunion. He comes out of prison and onto a throne, his brothers come begging for grain, and the boy they threw in a well finally says it: I am Yusuf. With full power to destroy them, he chooses seven words instead: no blame upon you today. Years before, Allah had sent this whole surah down to comfort the Prophet ﷺ in his year of grief, and years after, he ﷺ stood before the Quraysh who had exiled him and forgave them with Yusuf's exact words. Two prophets, one sentence, the same mercy. Retold faithfully from Mufti Ismail Menk's beloved series (the episode is embedded on the site), Qur'an verified via quran.ai.
