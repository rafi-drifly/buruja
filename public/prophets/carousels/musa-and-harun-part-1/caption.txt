Day 19 of 29: Musa and Harun, peace be upon them, part 1. Pharaoh claims to be god and slaughters the newborn boys of Bani Israil, so a mother is inspired to float her baby down the Nile, and Allah carries him straight into the palace that hunted him and back to his own mother's arms. This is the lesson this whole pillar keeps pointing at: the place that looks most like the end of you is often exactly where al-Latif, the Subtle, is most at work. The river meant to drown him became the road that saved him.
