Day 21 of 29: Musa and Harun, part 3, the confrontation and the sea. The magicians believe in an instant and die that day as martyrs; nine signs fall on Egypt; the Red Sea splits at the strike of a staff; and Pharaoh's faith arrives one breath too late, his body preserved as a sign (Surah Yunus 10:90-92). Same words of faith, two different endings, because faith is only faith while you can still refuse it. Allah recalls his drowning in Surah an-Naziat as a warning for us.
