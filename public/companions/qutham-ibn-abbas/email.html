The child who looked most like the Prophet ﷺ

Preview: He was the baby of a famous family, and he found his own quiet place in it.

Assalamu alaikum,

He was the baby of the family. His father, Abbas, was so beloved he was almost a father to the Prophet ﷺ. His brothers were leaders and scholars. It would have been easy for the youngest to be lost among them.

This is the story of Qutham ibn Abbas (may Allah be pleased with him).

Of the whole family, Qutham resembled the Prophet ﷺ the most. Several relatives were known to look like him, but some scholars held that Qutham was the most identical of all. He was only a small child in the Prophet's lifetime, so the likeness showed itself fully as he grew.

His mother once dreamed that a part of the Prophet ﷺ was in her house. He told her it meant she would nurse his grandson al-Husayn alongside her own son, and so Qutham and al-Husayn grew up as brothers through milk. Nearness to the Prophet ﷺ reached this family in ordinary, domestic moments of love.

When the Prophet ﷺ was buried, Qutham was given a distinction that is hard to imagine. He was the last person to come out of the grave. He placed the last stone over the body of the Messenger of Allah ﷺ before rising. Of everyone gathered, the quiet youngest son was the one who stayed longest.

He lived a long life and grew into a man of dignity. After one battle, a man tried to give him a thousand extra shares for his bravery. He refused. Put it back among the spoils, he said. Give it to the Muslims. I do not want it.

He carried his family's banner to the opening of Samarqand, far to the east, and died there as a martyr. Generations later, the great scholar Imam al-Bukhari would be born in that same region.

There is a lesson in him that is easy to miss: do not only watch the superstar. Look at what is being built quietly around him. A small life, given fully to Allah, is never lost.

May Allah be pleased with Qutham, and gather us in the company of the people he loved.
