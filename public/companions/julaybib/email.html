The companion no family would claim

Preview: We do not know his tribe, his region, or even his real name. The Prophet ﷺ knew him by heart.

Assalamu alaikum,

There is a companion whose tribe we do not know. We do not know his region either. We do not even know his real first name. He is remembered only by a nickname: Julaybib (may Allah be pleased with him).

He was poor. He had no standing in Medina. People mocked his appearance, and no family claimed him. By every measure his society used, he was a man easy to overlook.

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ did not overlook him. He would bring Julaybib forward, prefer him in his dealings, and draw him near. When it came time for marriage, the Prophet went himself to a family of the Ansar and proposed on Julaybib's behalf.

The mother refused aloud, and the Prophet could hear her. Then the daughter spoke. She asked if they were really going to turn the Prophet away, and she said: marry me to him, for the Prophet would never let me go to waste. She was from a notable family and knew the talk she would face, and she chose Julaybib anyway. They accepted, and the Prophet made a tender dua that Allah would pour good upon her life and never make it one hardship after another.

Later there was a battle. Julaybib, who had kept away from the harshness of others, did not keep away from the field. When it ended, the Prophet asked who was missing. The tribes counted their men and found them all. Then he said: but I am missing Julaybib. They searched and found his small body, with seven of the enemy he had killed lying around him.

The Prophet knelt over him and said, three times, "This one is from me, and I am from him." Because Julaybib was so small, the Prophet gathered him into his arms and buried him with his own hands. And the dua he had once made for his widow was answered: it was said that no widow among the Ansar gave more in charity than she did.

There is a lesson in him that is easy to miss: the regard of Allah is the only one that counts. The world saw a man with no tribe and no wealth. The Prophet saw someone who was from him.

May Allah be pleased with Julaybib, and gather us among those who are honoured in His sight.
