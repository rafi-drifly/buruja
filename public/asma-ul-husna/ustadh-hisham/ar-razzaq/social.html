Caption

When the headlines are all layoffs and rising prices, the worry has a way of creeping into your chest. This is the name for exactly that ache.

Ustadh Hisham Abu Yusuf opens Ar-Razzaq, the Provider, with a bird placing food into the open mouth of its chick: rizq is what is given to you, that keeps you alive. But it is so much wider than the food on your table. Guidance is provision. Love is provision. Paradise is provision. Someone with little money can be richer than everyone around them, because their real wealth is faith.

And the way He gives is unlike us. We budget every gift down to the pound; He provides without counting and without limit, like a blank cheque signed and handed to you. He brought Maryam fruit out of season with no shop in sight, and that one sight moved Zakariyya to ask for the son he had waited years for.

So loosen your grip. Trust Allah, not the lock on your door. Ask Him boldly, not for the bare minimum. And remember the greatest provision is not in the bank at all: it is a heart connected to Him.

Which provision will you thank Him for today? 🤍
