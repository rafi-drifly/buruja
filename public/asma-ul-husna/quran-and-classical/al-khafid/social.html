Al-Khafid.

The One who brings low. The Abaser.

We live in a world that looks upside down: the arrogant on top, the honest kept low. And you wonder if anyone ever sets it right.

This name does not answer gently.
01 / 09
First, an honest note.

Al-Khafid is not in the Qur'an as a definite name (الْخَافِض). What the Qur'an gives is the Day's own work:

'khafidatun rafi'a', it brings down [some] and raises up [others]. (56:3)

The name is how the tradition gathered that word.
02 / 09
And the Qur'an will not let you hold it alone.

In one breath: khafidah AND rafi'ah. Lowering AND raising.

Al-Qurtubi: 'the One who truly lowers and truly raises is Allah alone' (al-Khafid wa al-Rafi).

The Hand that drops the arrogant lifts the lowly.
03 / 09
Who exactly gets brought low?

as-Suddi: the arrogant (al-mutakabbirin), and it raised the humble.

Ibn Kathir: those mighty in the world (a'izza), down to the lowest, and the lowly (wuda'a) raised highest.

Ibn 'Ashur: the tyrants and the corruptors.
04 / 09
Watch it happen in history.

Abdullah ibn Ubayy sneered: 'the mightier will expel the lowlier' (63:8), meaning HE was mighty and the Prophet ﷺ lowly.

Allah flipped it: 'honor belongs to Allah, His Messenger, and the believers.'

He called himself highest. He was named lowest.
05 / 09
And the scariest part:

'but the hypocrites do not know' (63:8).

The arrogant are often the last to feel the fall. Certain they are rising, the whole time they are sinking.

The most dangerous fall is the one you take while sure you are climbing.
06 / 09
So how do you actually rise?

Not by climbing over people. 'Whoever wants honor, all honor belongs to Allah.' (35:10)

'The good word ascends to Him, and the righteous deed raises it.'

The most overlooked believer can rise this way. 🤍
07 / 09
Meet the companion name: Ar-Rafi, the Exalter.

'Rafi' al-darajat', high above all degrees. (40:15)

al-Sa'di: what lifts a servant's rank is sincerity (ikhlas), the deed for His face alone.

The Hand that lowers the arrogant reaches down to raise the sincere.
08 / 09
Ya Khafid, ya Rafi.

The arrogant look like they're winning. But you're reading the scene at halftime.

Raise me by Your obedience. Do not lower me by my own arrogance.

The world is being turned right way up, and the Day will show it. 🤍

Caption

We live in a world that looks upside down. The arrogant rise, the honest stay low, and you wonder if anyone ever sets it right.

Al-Khafid, the One who brings low, meets you there. One honest note first: this name does not appear in the Qur'an in its definite form. What the Qur'an gives is the Day's own work, 'khafidatun rafi'a,' it brings down some and raises up others (56:3). The name is how the classical tradition gathered that word, and al-Qurtubi states plainly that 'the One who truly lowers and truly raises is Allah alone.'

Notice the Qur'an speaks the lowering and the raising in one breath. The tradition always teaches Al-Khafid beside Ar-Rafi, the Exalter, because the same just Hand drops the arrogant and lifts the lowly. And the scholars name exactly who is brought low: as-Suddi says the arrogant (al-mutakabbirin), Ibn 'Ashur says the tyrants and the corruptors, Ibn Kathir says those who were mighty in the world, cast down even as the lowly are raised highest.

You can watch it happen in history. The hypocrite Abdullah ibn Ubayy sneered that 'the mightier will expel the lowlier' (63:8), certain he was the mighty one and the Prophet ﷺ the lowly. Allah overturned it in the next breath: honor belongs to Allah, His Messenger, and the believers. He called himself highest and was named lowest, and 'the hypocrites do not know' it, still feeling tall while sinking.

So how do you rise? Not by climbing over anyone. 'Whoever wants honor, all honor belongs to Allah' (35:10). The good word ascends to Him and the righteous deed raises it, and He raises the people of faith and knowledge by degrees (58:11). The most overlooked believer can rise this way.

The arrogant look like they are winning, but you are reading the scene at halftime. The world is being turned the right way up.

What would change if you truly believed the high and low you see now are not final? 🤍
