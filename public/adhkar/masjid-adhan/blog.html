The Masjid & the Adhan

أذكار المسجد والأذان
Answering the call to the house of Allah

Five times a day the call goes out, and a believer answers it. Between hearing the adhan and standing in the rows, there is a path of words the Prophet ﷺ walked: light asked for on the way, mercy sought at the door, and a quiet du'a that the heavens do not turn back.

These are the adhkar of the masjid and the call to prayer. They are how you answer the summons with your whole self, not just your feet.

Dhikr & Du'a

The Masjid & the Adhan

أذكار المسجد والأذان
Answering the call to the house of Allah

How to use these

When the adhan is called, repeat each phrase after the muadhdhin, except at 'come to prayer' and 'come to success', where you say the words of seeking help from Allah. After the adhan, send blessings on the Prophet ﷺ and ask for his station of intercession, then make your own du'a, for the time between the adhan and the iqamah is not turned away.

Say the entering du'a as you step into the masjid, leading with your right foot, and the leaving du'a as you step out, leading with your left.

2Entering the masjid

8Du'a between the adhan and the iqamah
يَدْعُو لِنَفسِهِ بَيْنَ الْأَذَانِ وَالْإِقَامَةِ فَإِنَّ الدُّعَاءَ حِينَئِذٍ لاَ يُرَدُّ

Between the call to prayer and the 'Iqamah, you should supplicate Allah for yourself. Invocation during this time is not rejected.

At-Tirmidhi, Abu Dawud, Ahmed · sahih
The Prophet ﷺ taught that supplication in this window is not rejected. Use it: ask for what weighs on your heart before the prayer begins.

The path to the prayer

Every step toward the masjid is written for you, one raising your rank and one wiping away a sin. To walk that path with these words on your tongue is to arrive already in worship, your heart turned before your body bows.

O Allah, open for us the doors of Your mercy, grant our Prophet ﷺ the station You promised him, and make us of those whose feet carry them to Your house in light.

Frequently asked

What do I say while the adhan is being called?

Repeat each phrase quietly after the muadhdhin. The one exception is at 'come to prayer' and 'come to success', where instead you say La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah (there is no might nor power except by Allah).

What should I say after the adhan finishes?

Send blessings on the Prophet ﷺ, then ask Allah to grant him al-wasilah and the honoured station. The Prophet ﷺ said whoever asks this for him will be granted his intercession on the Day of Resurrection. Then make your own du'a, as the moment between adhan and iqamah is answered.

Which foot do I lead with entering and leaving the masjid?

Enter leading with your right foot and say the du'a asking Allah to open the doors of His mercy. Leave with your left foot and ask Allah for His favour, since for honoured places the right is given precedence on the way in.

Why is the du'a between the adhan and iqamah special?

Because the Prophet ﷺ told us that supplication made in that window is not rejected. It is a short, easily missed opening in the day where the door of response is held open, so do not let it pass without asking.
