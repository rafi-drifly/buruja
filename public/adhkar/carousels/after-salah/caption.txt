The adhkar after salah.

Don't rush away after the salam. The Prophet ﷺ stayed a little longer: istighfar, the oneness of Allah, the tasbih of thirty-three, and Ayat al-Kursi, after which nothing stands between you and Paradise but death.

The sins of the one who keeps this, he said, are forgiven though they be like the foam of the sea.
