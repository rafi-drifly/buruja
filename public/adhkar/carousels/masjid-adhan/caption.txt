The adhkar of the masjid and the call to prayer, how you answer the summons with your whole self, not just your feet.

Repeat after the muadhdhin. Send blessings on the Prophet ﷺ and ask for his station of intercession. Then make your own du'a before the iqamah, the window the Prophet ﷺ said is never turned away. Enter with your right foot asking for mercy, leave with your left asking for His favour.
