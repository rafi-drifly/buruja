The morning adhkar, the wird of the believer at the start of the day.

Gathered from the Qur'an and the authentic sunnah: Ayat al-Kursi and the three Quls for protection, Sayyid al-Istighfar (the chief way of seeking forgiveness), the praise that hands the day back to Allah, and the tasbih that is light on the tongue and heavy on the scale.

You do not have to do all of it on day one. Take three and keep them. The most beloved deeds to Allah are the small and the constant.
