The du'as of the gathering, what to say while you sit and the words that seal it before you rise.

The Prophet ﷺ would quietly seek forgiveness again and again as he sat. And before leaving any sitting he taught the kaffarat al-majlis, the expiation that forgives whatever idle talk slipped in.

Both are short. Make the closing one a habit you reach for as you stand to go, so nothing has to be carried out of the room with you.
