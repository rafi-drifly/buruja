When doubt whispers: what the Prophet ﷺ taught.

His companions came to him troubled by thoughts they would rather fall from the sky than speak. He did not scold them. He told them that hating the whisper is itself 'clear faith.'

The response he taught is simple: seek refuge in Allah, then stop. Do not argue with the thought or chase it down. Turn away, affirm your belief, and keep your heart busy with His remembrance, the adhkar, Surah al-Baqarah in the home that Shaytan flees.
