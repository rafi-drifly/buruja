The adhkar of the pilgrim, station by station. From the moment you enter ihram, the rites move you from one place to the next, and at each one the Prophet ﷺ left words: the talbiyah of ihram, the remembrance of tawaf, the climb of Safa and Marwah, the great standing at Arafah, the night at Muzdalifah, and the stoning at the jamarat. The whole of Hajj is one long answer to a call. Labbayk Allahumma labbayk: here I am, again and again, from the first step to the last pebble.
