Istighfar: turning back to Allah.

Not only for after sin, but the constant turning of a heart that knows it can never thank Him enough. From the simple 'Astaghfirullah wa atubu ilayh' to Sayyid al-Istighfar, the chief of seeking forgiveness: say it with certainty and die that day, and you are among the people of Paradise.

Allah does not tire of forgiving. We only tire of asking.
