The evening adhkar.

The twin of the morning, recited from after Asr until sunset: Ayat al-Kursi for a guardian through the night, the three Quls for protection, Sayyid al-Istighfar, and the praise that hands the day back to the One who owns it.

Where the morning says 'we have entered the morning,' the evening says 'we have entered the evening.' Same trust, both ends of the day.
