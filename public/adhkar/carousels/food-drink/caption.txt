The du'as of food and drink.

A name at the start, praise at the end: Bismillah before you eat, alhamdulillah when you finish, a du'a for the one who fed you, and the words for breaking a fast.

None of it takes more than a breath. To begin in His name and end in His praise is to eat as a guest at a table you did not set.
