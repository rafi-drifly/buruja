Du'as for difficulty and distress, from the sunnah.

The du'a of distress (du'a al-karb), the du'a of Yunus by which the believer's call is answered, and the du'as for when you fear someone's harm or face injustice.

These are not slogans to push the feeling away. They take the weight to its proper place: into the hands of the One who alone can lift it. Say them when you have no other words left. They were given for exactly that.
