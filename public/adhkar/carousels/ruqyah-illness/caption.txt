Ruqyah for illness.

The heart of it is the Qur'an: al-Fatihah, Ayat al-Kursi, and the three Quls, recited into cupped hands and wiped over the body. Then the Prophet's du'as for the sick, for your own pain, and against the evil eye, which he told us is real.

Ruqyah is not instead of the doctor; it sits alongside the treatment. The Prophet ﷺ said Allah has sent down a cure for every illness.

And if the cure is delayed, the healing may be patience: no illness touches a believer without Allah washing away some of their sins through it.
