The du'as of travel. For the moment you take your seat, for setting out, for entering a new place, for parting from those you love, and for coming home. The riding du'a recites the verse: 'Glory to Him who subjected this to us, and we could not have subdued it ourselves.' Travel strips away the illusion that you are in charge. To set out with these words is to hand the journey to the One who can see its end.
