Du'as for nature and weather, the words the Prophet ﷺ said when the sky changed.

What to say when the wind rises and when thunder rolls, how to ask for rain and how to ask when there is too much of it, the words for the first drops and for after the rain passes, and the greeting for a new moon.

Most are a single line. Learn one or two and the ordinary sky becomes a reason to remember the One who holds it up.
