Du'as for death and grief.

Gathered gently in the order grief moves: how to comfort the one who is dying, what to say the moment they pass, the funeral prayer, burial, consoling the bereaved, and visiting the grave.

If all you can say is 'Inna lillahi wa inna ilayhi raji'un', we belong to Allah and to Him we return, that alone is enough. Allah knows what the heart cannot.

And the bond does not break at the grave: your du'a still reaches them. Keep making it.

Save this for when you, or someone you love, will need it. Full collection with Arabic, transliteration and meaning at buruja.com/adhkar/death-grief
