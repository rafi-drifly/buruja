The du'as the Prophet ﷺ carried. Not tied to a morning or a journey, these are the heart-supplications he reached for in every state of the soul: the famous plea for the one weighed down by anxiety, the refuge from worry and grief, what to do after you've sinned, and the wonder and gratitude that met good news. What they teach isn't a set of phrases but a habit of the heart: in grief you turn to Allah, in sin you turn back to Allah, in wonder you turn toward Allah.
