The adhkar before sleep.

The three Quls breathed into your hands and wiped over the body. Ayat al-Kursi, a guardian until morning. The last two verses of al-Baqarah. And the last words of the night, said on your right side, so that if you die before dawn, you die upon the fitrah.

Every night is a small rehearsal for the last one.
