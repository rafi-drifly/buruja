The du'as for money and the market.

The marketplace is where the heart is most easily pulled away: loud, busy, asking you to forget. So the sunnah gives you the du'a of the marketplace, with an immense reward for the one who remembers Allah where others forget Him. Alongside it: a plea that Allah make His lawful enough for you so you never reach for the forbidden, and refuge from the weight of debt.

Rizq is from Allah alone, and so is contentment with it.
