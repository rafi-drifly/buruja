How to pray istikharah, the prayer for seeking Allah's choice.

Jabir said the Prophet ﷺ taught them istikharah in all their affairs as he taught them a chapter of the Qur'an. It is not a last resort. It is the believer's first move when a real choice is in front of them.

The how: pray two rak'ahs of voluntary prayer, then raise your hands and recite the du'a, naming your decision. Ask Allah, if it is good for you, to bring it about and bless it; if it is bad, to turn it away and decree what is good instead. Then act, and trust the way He opens or closes.

The quiet relief: you no longer have to be certain you chose right. You have handed it to the One who sees every outcome.
