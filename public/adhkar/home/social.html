Leaving & Entering the Home
Words for the doorway
cover
There are words for the moment you walk out your front door.

Say them, and you step out guided, sufficed and protected.
the hook
بِسْمِ اللهِ ، تَوَكَّلْـتُ عَلى اللهِ وَلا حَوْلَ وَلا قُـوَّةَ إِلاّ بِالله
In the Name of Allah, I have placed my trust in Allah, there is no might and no power except by Allah.
a dhikr
Caption

The du'as for leaving and entering your home, words for a threshold you cross every single day.

The Prophet ﷺ said that whoever leaves with the du'a of trust is told: you are guided, defended and protected, and the devil turns away from him. And mentioning Allah's Name as you enter leaves the devil no place to lodge for the night.
