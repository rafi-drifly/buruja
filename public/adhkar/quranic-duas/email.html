Ask Allah in His own words

Preview: The Rabbana supplications: Adam, Yunus, Ibrahim, Musa, and the believers.

Assalamu alaikum,</p><p>When Allah wanted to teach us how to ask, He put the words in His own Book. The Qur'an is full of du'as: the cry of Adam after he slipped, of Yunus from inside the whale, of Ibrahim for his children, of ordinary believers asking for good in both worlds.</p><p>Every one of them was answered. Adam was forgiven. Yunus was lifted from the dark. Musa, who prayed alone and broke with nothing, had work and a home and a wife within days. To borrow their words is to join a line of the answered stretching back to the first human being.</p><p>We have gathered the Rabbana supplications here, each with Arabic, meaning, reference and recitation. Learn a few; they will come to you exactly when you need them:
